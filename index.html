Short Introduction

Being born and raised in a Caribbean paradise tends to warp your worldview significantly. I recall being quite young and sitting in the back seat as my parents drove through the mountains in Puerto Rico and being able to see the coastline from my vantage point, and thinking—ignorantly, of course—“that is the end of the world!”

Thinking back, I believe that, to some extent, that was true. All my experiences, my life events, my entire history was constrained by that ‘coastline’ by that barrier to the rest of the world. To me there was nothing more past that point.
